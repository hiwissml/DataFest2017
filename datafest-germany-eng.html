WELCOME TO DATAFEST GERMANY!

 

DataFest is a competition and interdisciplimary event at the same time, where you have the unique possibility to work with a large dataset and analyze them using your own ideas. In addition to that you have the possibility to easily get in contact with individual leaders and companies in the field of statistics.

 

DataFest 2017 is the third time that DataFest Germany organized this special event in Germany. It is organized by a consortium  consisting of the Chair of Statistic Chair of Statistics and Social Science Methodology at the University of Mannheim, the Institut of Statistics of LMU Munich and the P3 Group.

Under the roof of the American Statistical Association there are many more DataFests taking place in the USA. During the last years, our American partner universities and us, our experiences with this event has been very good.

 

The following links contain informatiton about the previous DataFests:

- DataFest 2016 in Munich

- DataFest 2015 in Mannheim

- DataFest at Duke University in the USA

WHAT WILL BE THE ASSIGNMENT?

 

The secret of what the provided datasets will contain is not disclosed until DataFest starts. But we can say that: It will be a big dataset of one of our partners from the business world. In the past, to give some examples, we provided data from the Los Angeles Police Department, which were analyzed to look for solutions in how to reduce crime rates, or data of a Dating-Website which were analyzed looking at which characteristics predicted the users' decisions who they will date.

During the time you work at the dataset, you are free to leave the working place and come back as you wish, as long as you do not work with the dataset. At least two members of your team need to be present at the beginning of the event.

 

Show your results in a short presentation to a diverse jury, consisting of experts from different professionl fields.

There are three categories, in which you can win:

  • Best Insight
  • Best Visualization
  • Best Use of Outside Data

SPONSORING

 

DataFest gives the enriching possibility to students to gain experiences analyzing real data and getting in touch with potential employers. Using the tool of a Career Fair, we want to support the mutual exploration. We encourage sponsors to visit DataFest as VIP Consultants at any time and not only witness the teams in action, but also accompany and counsell them during their process of analysis.

 

The sponsorship money help covering the direct expenses of the event, for example catering and advertising material. By helping us, you will support us with our mission, which is to promote the application of data to gain a better understanding of our environment, our social relationships and our physical and virtual self.

HOW CAN I PARTICIPATE?

 

Bachelor- and Master-students of all subjects, social and economic sciences, statistics, and IT students in particular, are welcomed to apply in teams of 2-5 people. People in your team can be friends, but also collegues or other companions. Because of the assignment's complexity, we recommend your team to cosist of three people at least.

 

If you still need members to form a team, you are free to join our DataFest-Group on Facebook, where you can team up with other interested.
